3 Emergency Features & Limitations

3.1 — SOS Broadcast Feature

The SOS feature transmits your GPS coordinates to your designated emergency contacts when you hold the SOS button and confirm activation. It does NOT:

  • Contact police, ambulance, fire, or any emergency services
  • Automatically dial any telephone number
  • Guarantee delivery of location to contacts (internet connectivity required)
  • Function as a substitute for calling your local emergency services directly
⚠ Critical Warning
In any life-threatening emergency, ALWAYS call your local emergency services directly. Do not rely on the SOS broadcast as your primary emergency response.

3.2 — Emergency Number Display

CrisisMatrix detects your approximate country using GPS to display the relevant local emergency telephone numbers. By using this feature you acknowledge:

  • Emergency numbers displayed are sourced from publicly available databases and may not be current or accurate
  • Emergency numbers can change — always verify through official government sources
  • CrisisMatrix makes no guarantee as to the accuracy, currency, or completeness of emergency number information
  • Country detection relies on GPS and may be inaccurate — you may manually override your detected country in the Profile screen

3.3 — Emergency Call Button

⚠ Important — Read Carefully
The 'Call Emergency Services' button opens your device's native telephone dialler pre-filled with the relevant emergency number. CrisisMatrix does NOT initiate, make, or complete a telephone call on your behalf. You are solely responsible for any call made using your device. 6 ARIA AI Assistant

6.1 — What ARIA Is

ARIA (AI Response Intelligence Assistant) is an AI-powered assistant powered by Anthropic's Claude large language model API. ARIA is subject to the transparency requirements of EU AI Act Article 50 (effective 2 August 2026).

ℹ EU AI Act Disclosure (Article 50)
You are interacting with an AI system. ARIA is an artificial intelligence assistant. It is not a human, licensed professional, or emergency responder. This disclosure is required by EU Regulation 2024/1689 (AI Act).

6.2 — Limitations You Accept

By using ARIA, you explicitly acknowledge:

  • ARIA may produce hallucinations — factually incorrect responses presented with apparent confidence
  • ARIA responses are not reviewed by licensed medical or emergency professionals before delivery
  • ARIA is not a licensed medical professional, emergency responder, or legal advisor
  • ARIA's knowledge has a training cutoff date and may not reflect current protocols
  • Offline ARIA responses use a local RAG knowledge base which may be incomplete
  • ARIA does not have real-time information, cannot assess your specific situation, and cannot see your environment
⚠ Warning
NEVER rely solely on ARIA in a life-threatening emergency. Always call your local emergency services first. ARIA is a supplementary reference tool, not a substitute for emergency services.

6.3 — Data Sent to Anthropic

When ARIA is used in online mode, your text queries are transmitted to Anthropic, PBC (San Francisco, USA) for processing. Anthropic processes this data under a Data Processing Agreement with CrisisMatrix. Anthropic does not use API queries for model training under the standard API tier. See our Privacy Policy and anthropic.com/privacy for details.
